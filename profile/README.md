## What it is

You can build the app. The wall is always the same: auth, payments, a database,
HTTPS — the boring, fiddly, easy-to-get-wrong plumbing. Bosh ships it for you,
correctly, on your own VPS — then gets out of the way so you and your AI build
the actual product on top.

## How it works

1. **Run one command** on a fresh Ubuntu / Debian VPS.
2. **Paste your Stripe + Google keys** into the wizard — it wires the paywall,
   sign-in, database and SSL.
3. **You're live** on your own domain, green padlock, working checkout.

Flip from Stripe test mode to real payments whenever you're ready — one click.

## Under the hood

Next.js · Stripe · Postgres · Docker · Caddy (automatic Let's Encrypt SSL),
deployed to your own VPS. You own the box and everything on it.
